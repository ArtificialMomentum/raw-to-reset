Day 1 Recipe — Morning Reset Bowl (Comfort Edition)

Warm, mineral-rich nourishment to ease you into rhythm

💧 Why This Fits Today

During menstruation or early reset days, your body craves minerals, warmth, and calm blood sugar. This bowl supports gentle rehydration, steady glucose, and the “I’m safe” signal your nervous system needs to start restoring energy. Think: comfort meets chemistry.

🥣 Base Ingredients

  • ½ cup cooked quinoa or steel-cut oats
  • ½ cup warm plant milk or water (mineral salt pinch optional)
  • 1 tbsp ground flax or chia for fiber + minerals
  • 1 scoop pea protein or collagen (optional)

🍓 Sweet Option

  • Top with ½ cup berries (anti-inflammatory)
  • 1 tbsp nut butter or crushed nuts for satiety
  • Drizzle of honey or maple (optional)

Comforting and bright — supports iron and serotonin without spikes.

🥬 Savory Option

  • Add sautéed greens (spinach, kale, zucchini)
  • 1 soft boiled egg or ¼ cup tofu for protein
  • Sprinkle sea salt + olive oil for minerals and fat balance

Grounding and mineral-dense — supports cramp relief and focus.

🕊 Notes for Your Rhythm

Eat slowly and warm. Pair with your electrolyte water before coffee for maximum steadiness. You’re not aiming for “perfect nutrition” — you’re re-establishing trust between your body and your schedule.

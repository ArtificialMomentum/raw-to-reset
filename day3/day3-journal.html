Day 3 — Journal Reflection

You’re not lazy — you need fuel

💬 Grounding Note

Today is about grace. If your energy dipped or focus vanished, that’s not weakness — it’s rhythm in motion. Your body’s chemistry just changed lanes. The goal isn’t to “push through” but to support yourself like you would a friend who’s tired. Offer patience, fuel, and space instead of guilt.

🍽 Energy Check-In

Where did my energy feel strongest today? Where did I hit an invisible wall?

💗 Self-Talk Audit

What did my inner voice say when I slowed down or couldn’t keep pace? Would I speak to a friend that way?

🌿 Support Action

What nourishment — food, movement, rest, or boundary — will help me refuel tomorrow?

When you’re finished reflecting, you can share your thoughts or keep them just for you.
